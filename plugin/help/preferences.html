Plug-in Preferences

The global options for the Zeus plug-in are located in Eclipse's main preference dialog (Window -> Preferences) under the Zeus section.
The main preference page contains SOAP and debugging options for the Zeus plugin.

SOAP Update Interval

The SOAP update interval controls how much the plug-in contacts Zeus to update its internal state. The plug-in keeps a copy of all rules synchronised in local memory, therefore decreasing the update interval will make the plug-in notice rule changes on a Zeus faster (though it will also slightly increase the load on the Zeus admin server).

Support

These settings are used to debug problems in the plug-in, and should normally be turned off. Turning them on will slow down operations performed by the plug-in.
